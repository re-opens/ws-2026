Future Organizing Committee

RE-OpenS is designed as a community-driven workshop. Future organizing committees will be composed of researchers who actively contribute to REFSQ’s Open Science Initiative or demonstrate strong engagement with open science practices in requirements engineering. We aim to include diverse perspectives and invite new contributors through open calls and feedback from previous workshop participants.
