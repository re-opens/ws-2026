Future Organizing Committee

RE-OpenS is designed as a community-driven workshop. Future organizing committees will include researchers who actively contribute to REFSQ’s Open Science Initiative or demonstrate strong engagement with open science practices in requirements engineering. We aim to ensure diversity and continuity by inviting new contributors through open calls and incorporating feedback from previous workshop participants.
