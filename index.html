Welcome to RE-OpenS

RE-OpenS is the 1st Interactive Workshop on the Promotion of Open Science in Requirements Engineering, co-located with REFSQ 2026 in Poznań, Poland.

Open science is essential for addressing the replication crisis and fostering transparent, accessible, and replicable research. RE-OpenS supports this mission by introducing researchers to REFSQ’s Open Science Initiative, including its Open Science Track, Open Science Policy, and Open Science Competition.

This half-day, paperless workshop combines theoretical insights with hands-on practice. Participants will:

  • Learn about open science principles and REFSQ’s open science initiative
  • Gain practical experience using tools like SciKGTeX, ORKG, and ORKG Ask
  • Create FAIR-annotated publications and ORKG comparisons
  • Engage in a feedback session to shape future editions of RE-OpenS

RE-OpenS is designed for all REFSQ attendees interested in open science. Whether you're new to the topic or already contributing, this workshop offers valuable skills, networking opportunities, and a chance to influence the future of open science in RE.
